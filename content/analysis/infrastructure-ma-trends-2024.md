## Overview

The infrastructure M&A market continues to demonstrate resilience in 2024, driven by several secular themes including the energy transition, digital infrastructure expansion, and increasing demand for essential services.

## Key Trends

### Energy Transition Dominance

Renewable energy and energy transition assets continue to attract significant capital. Transactions involving wind, solar, battery storage, and grid infrastructure represent a growing share of total deal volume.

### Digital Infrastructure Growth

Data centers, fiber networks, and tower assets remain highly sought-after as data consumption accelerates globally. The AI revolution has further increased demand for digital infrastructure.

### Water and Environmental Services

Water infrastructure and environmental services have emerged as a growing area of investor focus, driven by aging infrastructure, regulatory requirements, and sustainability mandates.

## Market Outlook

Deal activity is expected to remain robust through the remainder of 2024, supported by substantial dry powder in infrastructure funds and continued privatization activity globally.
