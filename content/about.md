## About Infrastructure M&A Tracker

This site tracks mergers and acquisitions in the global infrastructure sector, including:

- **Energy** - Power generation, transmission, distribution, and renewables
- **Transport** - Airports, ports, toll roads, and rail
- **Digital Infrastructure** - Data centers, fiber networks, and telecom towers
- **Water & Utilities** - Water treatment, distribution, and waste management
- **Social Infrastructure** - Hospitals, schools, and government buildings

## Sectors Covered

We monitor transaction activity across all major infrastructure sub-sectors, with a focus on institutional-scale deals involving infrastructure funds, sovereign wealth funds, pension funds, and strategic buyers.
