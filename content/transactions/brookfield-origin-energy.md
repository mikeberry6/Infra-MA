## Transaction Overview

Brookfield Asset Management, in consortium with MidOcean Energy, completed the acquisition of Origin Energy, one of Australia's largest integrated energy companies. The deal represents one of the largest infrastructure transactions in the Asia-Pacific region.

## Strategic Rationale

The acquisition provides Brookfield with a significant platform in Australia's energy transition, including Origin Energy's substantial renewable energy pipeline and its position as a leading energy retailer.

## Key Terms

- Total enterprise value of approximately $18.7 billion AUD
- Brookfield acquired Origin Energy's energy markets business
- MidOcean Energy acquired the integrated gas business (including APLNG stake)

## Regulatory Considerations

The transaction required approval from the Australian Foreign Investment Review Board (FIRB), the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC), and Origin Energy shareholders.
