## Transaction Overview

Global Infrastructure Partners has announced the acquisition of Suez Water Technologies & Solutions, a global leader in water treatment and process solutions.

## Strategic Rationale

The acquisition targets a critical infrastructure sector benefiting from increasing global demand for water treatment and sustainability-focused solutions. The deal positions GIP as a major player in the water infrastructure space.

## Key Terms

- Enterprise value of approximately $4.2 billion
- Transaction includes global water treatment operations
- Expected to close subject to regulatory approvals

## Regulatory Considerations

The transaction is subject to customary regulatory approvals including antitrust review in the EU and other jurisdictions.
