## Transaction Overview

KKR completed the acquisition of fiber infrastructure assets from April Group, expanding its digital infrastructure portfolio in the Asia-Pacific region.

## Strategic Rationale

The transaction aligns with KKR's strategy to invest in critical digital infrastructure assets that benefit from secular tailwinds including increasing data consumption and the need for fiber connectivity.

## Key Terms

- Acquisition of fiber-optic network assets across multiple markets
- Long-term contracted revenue streams from enterprise and wholesale customers
